About EduPortal

EduPortal is a revolutionary educational platform that harnesses the power of AI to transform how students learn. By analyzing your uploaded content - whether it's PDFs, YouTube videos, or lecture recordings - we create personalized, multilingual notes tailored to your learning style. Our comprehensive suite includes progress tracking, adaptive quizzes, and an intelligent chatbot tutor, all designed to make learning more effective, accessible, and enjoyable for students worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EduPortal

How does the note generation work?

+

Simply upload your PDFs, paste YouTube links, or upload lecture files. Our AI analyzes the content and generates personalized notes in your preferred format - descriptive, short, or formula-based.

Can I customize the tone of my notes?

+

Yes! You can choose from professional, casual, example-based, or other moods to match your learning style. The AI adapts the language and examples accordingly.

Is the platform accessible for visually impaired students?

+

Absolutely! We offer audio conversion of all notes, screen reader compatibility, and voice-based interactions to ensure accessibility for all students.

How many languages are supported?

+

Our platform supports over 50 languages for both note generation and chatbot interactions, making education accessible globally.

How does the progress tracking work?

+

The system tracks your quiz performance, study time, and topic mastery. It creates visual graphs and suggests personalized study schedules based on your strengths and areas for improvement.
